1 in 8 Americans don’t know where their next meal will come from. This is Food Insecurity.


Swipe Out Starvation is an organization concerned with food insecurity, localy and globally.


We donate unused meal swipes from Purdue students to local and global charities.

Our Mission:

To promote awareness and prompt action by allowing Purdue students to make a meaningful contribution in the fight against hunger.

How It Works

1

Choose How Much

We have donation cards at every On-The-Go checkout. Simply give as many donation cards as you want to the cashier.

Side = 1 Donation = 25¢
Meal = 4 Donation Cards = $1

2

You're Done

Now feel good knowing that $1 from you will feed a family of 3.

Where Donations Go

Local

Food Finders is a food bank in Lafayette, IN committed to reducing food insecurity and fighting hunger.

Global

Land of a Thousand Hills is a Rwandan coffee plantation aiming to rebuild Rwanda after its 1994 genocide.

On average, Swipe out Starvation donates $5000 per semester to both Food Finders and Land of a Thousand Hills
