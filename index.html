WHERE DONATIONS GO

LOCAL

Food Finders is a food bank in Lafayette, IN committed to reducing food insecurity and fighting hunger.

GLOBAL

Land of a Thousand Hills is a Rwandan coffee plantation aiming to rebuild Rwanda after its 1994 genocide.

On average, Swipe out Starvation donates $5000 per semester to both Food Finders and Land of a Thousand Hills.
