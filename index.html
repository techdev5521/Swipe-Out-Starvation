1 in 8 Americans don’t know where their next meal will come from. This is Food Insecurity.


Swipe Out Starvation is an organization concerned with food insecurity, localy and globally.


We donate unused meal swipes from Purdue students to local and global charities.

Our Mission:

To promote awareness and prompt action by allowing Purdue students to make a meaningful contribution in the fight against hunger.

How It Works

You're Done

Now feel good knowing that $1 from you will feed a family of 3.
